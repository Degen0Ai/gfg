About Us

Your Home,
Our Expertise

Ronne Home Solutions is a West Michigan-based remodeling and handyman service proudly serving communities from the lakeshore all the way to Grand Rapids. Founded on a background in house flipping, we bring a deep, practical understanding of what it takes to transform any space — efficiently and beautifully.

Every project, large or small, receives the same level of attention and craftsmanship. We take the time to understand your vision, present the best options for your specific situation, and see it through to the finish — on time, every time.

All work is fully licensed and insured, giving you complete peace of mind from start to finish.
